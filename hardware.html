Motherboard is much like the spine of your computer. It is what links all the different components inside
your computer together so that they can function.The Motherboard itself is a printed circuit board that
allows the CPU, RAM, .and all other computer hardware components to communicate with each.

CPU is short for central processing unit. It's mainly a piece of hardware that carries out the instructions
of a computer program.

RAM is what the computer takes from the hard drive and stores it. This helps give you faster access to things.

The hard drive is where everything thats in your computer gets stored. For example, your photos,documents, pictures,
music, programs, and preferences.

The BUS connects all the internal components of a computer. It is a communication system that
transfers data between components inside a computer, or between computers

Computer keyboards are an arrangement of "keys" which have letters, numbers, and other things
printed on them to help you write.

A mouse is a device that controls the movement of the cursor on a display screen. It is a small
object you can roll along a hard, flat surface.

A monitor is an electronic visual display for computers. What it does is, for example, if you are watching a video
the monitor is what will display it so you can actually be able to see it
